Valleys Notes

07 May 2014

Some highlights from Valley's recent games

Valley's Attack

I'm quite impressed with Valley's attack. They are fast, they chase everything and they are quite dangerous when they get into space. A lot of young kids in their forward line so they will run all day too.

Fast break up the line. Ball travels back infield where it is contested by a fullback. The ball is then slapped into the circle where a forward one times it past the keeper.

Their corners are decent too. They seem to favour two variations, the straight flick and the layoff to L1. They also do a good job of getting in around the rebound.

Straight flick from the top aimed at the left postie, about waste height. Ball is deflected in.

Layoff to L1. Straight flick along the ground is saved by the keepers feet. Cleared by a defender.

Valley's Defence

This is their real weakness. Their defenders are afraid of the ball. Watch how they dive out of the way as an opponent winds up for a shot. If you get enough space to have a swing in the circle the defenders will get out of your way.

Uni player works the ball into the circle one on one. Shoots through two valleys defenders who dive out of the way.

Penalty at the top of the circle is played back. Uni player drifts to the left and hits into the circle. Another uni player bumps the ball towards the back line and hits a tomahawk shot at goal. The ball goes through the keepers legs.

Their defensive corners aren't any better. The layoff to L1 gets very little pressure from the defence.

Layoff to L1. Straight flick along the ground to the keepers stick side. Saved by the stick side postie. The injector pounced on the rebound to score.
