Easts Defensive Corners

20 Mar 2014

Videos from last year of Easts defensive corner battery

Corner by Commercial that resulted in a goal. Straight flick from the top goes along the ground to the keepers stick side. Keeper gets a touch but it goes in.

  • For this corner three of the Easts defenders come a long way out. That will leave room in behind for us to get the best positions for rebounds.
  • Butterini is usually very solid on his stick side. I think he is distracted by the attempted deflection. He ends up trying to make the save with his foot and his stick.

Corner by Kedron that resulted in a goal. Straight hit goes through the postie to score.

  • Good hit. Their postie didn't look comfortable playing at the ball at all.

Corner by Bulimba that was saved. Hit straight from the top. Keeper saves lying down with his gloves. Rebound is missed by injector.

  • See how the keeper tries to deflect the rebound towards the right postie. If the injector had gotten his shoulder in front he likely would have earned another corner.
  • Alternatively if he had hung back a meter or two he could have pounced on the stick side postie as he tried to control the rebound. This is what I think you should do Andrew.

Corner by Commercial that was saved. Bunt to L1 leading to a shot low to the keepers glove side. Rebound goes clear out to the attackers left side of the field.

  • From the looks of this the Easts sitck side postie will head out to Josh when we go one left. He is also a bit of a wimp as he is putting no pressure on the flicker at all. He is well outside the post.
  • Andrew look at where the rebound goes (which comes off the second runner not the keeper). If you stay out at the runners mark you will have a much greater chance of regathering these sort of rebounds. If the rebound goes elsewhere then you need to get into the post as you will be wide open.
  • Josh, this shows the trade off between flicking left or right. If you go left the keeper has a good site on the shot, if you go right there will be a keeper, postie and second runner all playing at it. There is no right answer. Just get them on target, some will go in and we will scrap for the rebounds.
