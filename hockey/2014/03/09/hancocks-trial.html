Commercial Trial Videos

09 Mar 2014

Video from the trial against Commercial

We managed to get some video from the trial match against Commercial. Due to technical difficulties we only got the last 6min of the first half and about half of the second half. I've put links to the videos at the bottom of this post. I've picked out a few specific bits that are worth discussing. By default the videos show up at a low resolution but if you click on the gear you can get a higher resolution that is much better to view.

This first clip shows a Commercial long corner. This shows a pattern that Commercial use a lot. Their center half lead out towards our inside forward. This drags Sam out of the center and allows their inside to lead into the space left behind. Sam reads the pass this time which is why they didn't get a shot but this is one of the ways in which Commercial are able to get extra, unmarked players into dangerous positions. At the start of this movement Andrew, Ian and Sam are marking Commercials inside and center half. By the end Commercial have an unmarked player on the top of our circle and Andrew and Ian are not marking anybody.

This clip shows one of Commercials goals.

  • Lots of unmarked players
  • Lack of urgency in defence
  • Poor organisation from the back.
  • Commercial use a one, two to get past Sam
  • The same two players use a one, two to get past Hawley
