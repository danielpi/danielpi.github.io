UQ Strengths

26 Mar 2014

Videos from last year of UQ's Strengths

16 yard hit is passed out wide. A missed tackle and a couple of passes lead to a cross to the top of the circle. An unmarked center forward gets a shot on goal that is saved by the keeper.

  • UQ set up with three across the back a lot. Ashley Hennesy is always in the center and he can throw a good pass.
  • UQ make some meters by getting on the outside with Brownhalls, but the run becomes dangerous when the winger is able to get back on the inside of his man.

Inside forward intercepts 16 yard hit. Returns and passes into the circle for a shot.

  • UQ's inside forwards look for a lot of intercepts. Brownhalls in particular generates a lot of turnovers for them.

Penalty outside the 25 leads to a hit straight at the goal. Two deflections and the ball is in the net.

  • UQ attempt a lot of deflections. For hits outside the 25 they will stick a player next to the closest defender and hit through their own player. They will then run two forwards at each post and hope for the best. A lot of the goals look flukey but they happen quite regularly.
  • Hennesey and Rintala are particularly good at throwing the passes.

Great goals from the jake hathaway

  • The lead up to this goal is pretty slopey but I put it in to show that we need to mark jake Hathaway tightly. From watching the videos I would rate him as one of the best forwards in the comp last year.
