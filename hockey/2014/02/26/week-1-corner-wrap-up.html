Week 1 Corner wrap up

26 Feb 2014

A reasonable first practice run for the corners. On the whole the pushouts and traps were generally good. Most corners got to a shot which is what we are aiming for. Most of the shots were at the goal too which was excellent. A couple of points to work on this week though are

  • We didn't work very hard on gathering the rebounds. Every corner is an opportunity to put pressure on a defence.
  • Our straight flick and layoff were very easy to pick. The shooter on the layoff needs to really look like they expect the ball right up until the ball has already been flicked from the top.
  • Our layoff position wasn't consistent from person to person. I don't think we were wide enough at times. We can't afford for the first runner to be able to block the shot from L1.
